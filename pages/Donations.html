    Who you are?

    Madrasa SHAH HAMDAN is a school of Islamic jurisprudence that emphasizes the Muslim Unity. Its very foundations rests on the belief in Allah, Angels, Prophets, Day of Judgement, Holy Quran and other Holy Islamic Scriptures revealed upon previous Prophets. While, practices include Prayers (five times in a day) Fasting of Ramadan, Zakah and Pilgrimage journey to Kaaba. These Beliefs and Practices has been excerpted from the books: Usool Aitaqadia(deals with Beliefs) and Fiqh ul Ahwat (deals with Islamic Jurisprudence), which were written by Hazrat Shah Syed Muhammad Nurbaksh.

    What is the vission

    To provide every opportunity for an all round and harmonious development of individuals and society and reconstruction of human thought in all its forms on the foundations of Islam in order to encourage and promote education, training the research in Islamic learning, social, natural, applied and communication sciences and other branches of learning to ensure the Muslim Ummah’s ideological, moral, intellectual, social, economic and technological development in accordance with the values, ideals, principles and norms of Islam.

    Whats is the objectives of instition

     To organize Noorbakhshis globally. To strive for the protection of religious rights of Noorbakhshis. To establish a welfare Islamic society by promoting unity among the Islamic Ummah. To continue the glorious mission of Hazrat Shah Hamdan Amir Kabir Syed Ali Hamdani A.R and his spiritual successor Hazrat Shah Syed Muhammad Noorbakhsh A.R. To streamline religious publications and strive to regulate other fields of propagation and preach Islamic tenets globally. To struggle for the protection and progress of Muslim Ummah and the Islamic Republic of Pakistan. To provide a strong plate form for Noorbakhshis especially youth for their intellectual and spiritual grooming To take steps for the implementation of Shariat-e-Muhammadia on universal basis by developing linkages with the followers of Fiq-ul-Ahwat, living in different countries.
